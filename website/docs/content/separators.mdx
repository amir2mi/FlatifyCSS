With separators, content can have an area to breathe, suitable whitespace, or anything you call it.  
Three kinds of separators are available in Flatify CSS, dots, line, and short line.

### Dots

Three gorgeous dots, that is all a long document needs. The opacity of the separators primarily are decreased, it helps element to tread more as a passive object in the playground, you can use [opacity helper classes](/docs/helpers/opacity-setters) to change it.

```html title="index.html"
<hr class="dots opacity-90" />
<hr class="dots color-red size-sm" />
<hr class="dots style-blue opacity-100" />
```
